Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

What Known Is

Known (finallyknown.ai) is a memory system for AI agents. It stores observations about you — extracted from your conversations with AI tools — so your agents can understand you better over time.

What Data We Collect

  • Conversation extractions: When you use an AI tool connected to Known, we extract observations (preferences, patterns, facts) from your conversations. We do NOT store full conversation transcripts by default — only extracted trait codes, personal facts, and verbatim paragraphs relevant to understanding you.
  • Raw message text: When you use the ingest endpoint, the full text is processed by our LLM and stored for grep/expand functionality. This text is only accessible with your API key.
  • Facts you explicitly store: When you tell your agent "remember this," the exact text is stored.
  • Device identifiers: A hashed device ID is used to auto-provision your account. We do not collect your name, email, IP address, or browser fingerprint.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name or email (unless you provide it for billing)
  • Your IP address
  • Browser cookies or browsing history
  • Data from any tool you haven't explicitly connected to Known
  • Passwords, API keys, or sensitive credentials (these are filtered during extraction)

Where Data Is Stored

  • Database: Turso (SQLite at the edge), hosted on AWS US-West-2
  • Compute: Cloudflare Workers (global edge network)
  • Embeddings: Generated by Cloudflare Workers AI — processed in-flight, not stored separately
  • LLM processing: OpenAI GPT-4o-mini — conversation text is sent to OpenAI for extraction, subject to OpenAI's API data usage policy (not used for training)

Who Can Access Your Data

  • You: Only your API key can read your data. No one else can query your brain.
  • Third-party apps: Only if you explicitly grant access via the /access/grant endpoint. You can revoke access at any time.
  • Known team: We can access the database for debugging and support. We will never sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

How to Delete Your Data

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Delete specific memories: Tell your agent "forget [topic]" or call DELETE /forget
  • Delete everything: Call DELETE /forget with {"what": "*"} to wipe your entire brain
  • Delete your account: Email will@finallyknown.ai and we'll delete all data within 48 hours
  • Disconnect a device: Remove the API key from your tool's configuration

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Cancelled accounts: Data is retained for 90 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted
  • Free accounts: Inactive accounts (no API calls for 12 months) may be deleted with 30 days notice

Chrome Extension

The Known Chrome Extension observes conversations on supported AI sites (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity) and sends extracted text to Known Cloud for processing. The extension:

  • Only activates on supported AI sites listed in the manifest
  • Does not access your browsing history, cookies, or other tabs
  • Stores only your API key locally (in chrome.storage.local, encrypted by Chrome)
  • Can be disconnected at any time via the popup

Children

Known is not intended for use by anyone under 13 years of age.
