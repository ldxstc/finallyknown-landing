What changes

Will had been using his AI agent for a year. He'd built three products with it. His memory file had grown to 4,000 lines. He pruned it weekly. His agent kept forgetting things anyway.

Then Known noticed something Will had never seen.

He was systematically avoiding the one conversation that could change his career. Every time he got close to it, a new project appeared. Four times in three months.

His agent said:

"You've done this four times. The conversation with your CEO is the highest-leverage thing you can do this quarter. Everything else is displacement activity."

Will had the conversation the next morning.

That insight didn't exist in any file. No amount of manual memory management would have found it. Known discovered it by watching the pattern across hundreds of sessions.

How it works

Install once.
It handles everything.

Automatic

Learns from every conversation

After every session ends, Known extracts what matters — preferences, patterns, facts — into a local knowledge graph. No manual writing required. Ever.

Persistent

Survives everything

Session resets. Gateway updates. New agents. Known's brain is independent of all of it. What you taught your agent today, it knows tomorrow. And next year.

Intelligent

Finds what files can't

Known discovers behavioral patterns across your conversations — things you never explicitly said. Only what's watched over time can be seen. Files can't do this.

知己
知己

Known comes from 知己 (ちき / zhī jǐ) — a Japanese and Chinese concept of the person who truly knows you. Not a friend. Not a partner. Someone who understands you so deeply they can finish your thoughts.

"人生得一知己足矣" — In life, having one zhiji is enough.
