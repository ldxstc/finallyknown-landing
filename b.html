Under the hood

Three systems. One brain.

Encode

Learns from every conversation

After every session, Known extracts what matters — preferences, patterns, facts, relationships — into a knowledge graph. Two-pass extraction grounded in neuroscience. No manual writing required.

Activate

Surfaces what's relevant

Before every response, Known injects only the context that matters — using spreading activation, the same mechanism your brain uses to connect related memories. No stale context. No token waste.

Dream

Discovers what you can't see

In the background, Known connects distant patterns across your conversations — the way your brain makes connections during sleep. Insights strengthen with repeated discovery. The best ones surface without you asking.

Known comes from 知己 (ちき / zhī jǐ) — a Japanese and Chinese concept of the person who truly knows you. Not a friend. Not a partner. Someone who understands you so deeply they can finish your thoughts.

"人生得一知己足矣" — In life, having one zhiji is enough.
